# Early Adoption Plan

1. Demonstrate growth in the project’s community, including
   1. Growth in the number of commits to the project, the number of project committers, and the organizational diversity of contributions and committers.
      1. Update short-courses and college course syllabus.
      2. Engage and actively support projects that need modeling/simulation tools.
      3. Increase support and interaction with other LF Energy projects with similar/complimentary capabilities/needs.
   2. Production or planned production use of the project by at least two independent end users, which, in the TAC’s judgment, are of adequate quality and scope.
      1. Current in use and participation by CEC, Hitachi, NREL, and Livermore.
2. Technical Governance of the project is operational, as measured by:
   1. A Technical Steering Committee with at least five members and a chairperson elected by the members, holding regular open meetings (aligns with OpenSSF Best Practices Gold badge requirement)
      1. The TSC currently has 12 members, 4 of whom are voting members, and 2 addition emeritus members.
   3. Achievement of the OpenSSF Best Practice badge at the ‘Silver’ Level
      1. See https://www.bestpractices.dev/en/projects/11254?criteria_level=1 for checklist of requirements.
3. Development of a growth plan, to be done in conjunction with their project mentor(s) at the TAC. This plan should address the following points:
   1. Release plans for the next 18 months.
      1. Releases are schedule roughly every 2-3 months.
   3. Target end-users.
      1. Students, faculty, researchers, and utility engineers marketing strategy development needed.
   5. Identification of any regulatory or standards body requirements for deployment and implementation plans.
      1. List of applicable standards needed.
   7. Plans for the growth of project contributors and committers to support the growth plan.
      1. See Task 3.3 "Target end-users".
   9. Identification of any infrastructure resources needed to fulfill the growth plan.
      1. No specific additional infrastructure needs identified at this time.
